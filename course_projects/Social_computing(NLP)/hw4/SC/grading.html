Grading and Workload

Grading will be based on:

  • Five assignments: to be completed individually (50%)

  • Final project: individual or group of two students (25%): proposal (10%), final report (15%)

  • Exam (25%): center around lecture notes and is open-book.

  • Extra credit (see Policies for details)

Submission details for assignments (AST), project proposal (PRP), and final report (PRR) can be found on Blackboard.
